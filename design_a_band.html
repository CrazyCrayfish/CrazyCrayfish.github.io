Challenge: Design a Band

Challenge Information

  • - Design thinking is thinking up of something designed to please the audience. It teaches us how to emphasize with the user.
  • - We can do this through A, B testing, where we show two side by side examples and ask people which one is better and rose, bud, and thorn testing which asks for pros, cons, and new ideas you come up with.
  • - To create this album cover, we first use A, B testing to determine the font and color. We used randomized links to Wikipedia to determine the artist and album names. Finally, we used rose, bud and thorn testing to compare to versions of our covers.

Band Biography

It all began in the Karadeniz Technical University, in the Historical Emos class. The teacher was talking about the type of music that emos of the past listened to. Turns out they listened to classical and rock. My friends and I thought, "Why couldn't they just listen to one type of music?" We all had backgrounds in music. We got together and created a mix of classical and rock so that emos won't be torn against classical and rock. Buy our album for $5.99.
